**Research Focus**: My primary area of interest lies in exploring the application of mathematical and statistical frameworks such as **Stochastic Differential Equations (SDEs)**, **Stochastic Optimal Control (SOC)**, and **Sequential Monte Carlo (SMC)**. By establishing links between these frameworks and their practical implementations in the domains of machine learning and artificial intelligence (ML/AI), I aim to contribute to the advancement of these fields.
